Low Processing Rates

Complete Merchant Services offers the most competitive rate program in the industry today Interchange Pass Through, providing the lowest overall cost of service.

Most merchant account providers quote a "rate as low as" rate, this rate is generally what they call their Qualified Rate. Most tiered merchant accounts have a base rate plus two additional tiers that carry a surcharge when the type of card or acceptance method does not fall into the qualified tier. In this type of pricing method you are paying well above the interchange cost for all surcharged transactions.
