First Data FD55

The First DataT FD55 terminal is an economical point-of-sale terminal that enables merchants to accept virtually every merchant services payment option. This space-saving device is compatible with many standard peripheral devices. With the addition of optional equipment, the FD55 terminal can also accept paper or electronic checks.

The FD55 terminal has the latest fraud detection and prevention technologies built right in to keep your customers safe and your business compliant with industry guidelines. First Data's new TransArmorï¿½ solution offers a layer of protection previously unavailable anywhere on the market. This innovation combines encryption with tokenization, which completely removes card information from your environment so it cannot be used for fraud.

At a speed of 18 lines per second, the device's built-in printer powers through text for fast checkouts. It's built for jam-free operation, and it's a snap to reload-just open the cover and drop in the standard-sized credit card paper roll.
