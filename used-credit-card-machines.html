Used Credit Card Machines

We strive to provide our merchants with as many options as possible to increase their sales and reduce costs. Our used credit card machine selection provides many opportunities to businesses on a budget.

We carry used credit card machines that offer multiple connectivity options, such as TCP/IP terminals which utilize your business's existing high-speed Internet connection to process transactions in as little as three to five seconds. For lower-volume locations we also offer standard dial-up terminals which require the use of a phone line.
